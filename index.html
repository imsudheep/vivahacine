WHAT TO EXPECT

YOUR STORY,
step by step

Booking a filmmaker is a big decision. Here's exactly what happens — so you never feel lost, rushed, or unsure.

01

We sit down and talk

Not a sales call. A real conversation. We want to know how you met, what makes you laugh, what you're nervous about. The more we understand you, the more honest your film becomes.

02

We build the day around you

We figure out the light, the locations, the quiet moments worth protecting. We talk to your planner, scout the venue, and build a loose rhythm — so the day feels effortless, not orchestrated.

03

We disappear into the day

You won't hear "look here" or "do that again." We move quietly, reading the room, chasing the light and the laughter. The best moments are the ones nobody planned.

04

We craft something that lasts

Every frame is hand-edited, color-graded with intention, and woven into a story that feels like you. No templates. No batch processing. Just your film, treated like it matters — because it does.

05

We present it with care

Your film lives in a private, beautifully designed gallery — easy to share, impossible to forget. We also design custom albums and prints if you want something you can hold.
