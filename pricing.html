QUESTIONS

PRICING clarified

Is there a payment plan available?

Yes. We offer flexible payment schedules tailored to your timeline. A 30% retainer reserves your date, with the remaining balance split into two or three payments leading up to your wedding.

What is not included in the package pricing?

Travel and accommodation for destinations outside Tamil Nadu are quoted separately. Additional hours, second shooters, albums, and cinematography are available as add-ons.

Can I upgrade my package after booking?

Absolutely. You can add hours, services, or team members at any time leading up to your wedding, subject to availability. We'll adjust the investment accordingly.

Do you offer custom quotes for multi-day events?

Yes. Multi-day Indian weddings, destination celebrations, and weekend-long events often require tailored coverage. Reach out and we'll build a custom proposal.
