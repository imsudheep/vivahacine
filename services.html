COMMON QUESTIONS

EVERYTHING you need TO KNOW

How far in advance should we book?

Most couples book 8–12 months ahead. We recommend reaching out as soon as you have a venue and date locked—especially for peak-season or destination weddings where availability is limited.

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes—travel is at the heart of what we do. Based in Tamil Nadu, we cover weddings, elopements, and editorial projects across India. Travel fees are transparent and discussed upfront.

Can we combine photography and cinematography?

Absolutely. Most of our clients book both. We offer integrated packages with coordinated teams so that the photographic and cinematic coverage complement each other seamlessly.

How long does delivery take?

Full films and photograph galleries are delivered within 8–12 weeks. Highlight edits arrive sooner. Every frame is hand-edited and graded—we never rush the craft.

Can we customise a package?

Every package is a starting point. We tailor coverage hours, team size, deliverables, and add-ons like fine-art albums or second shooters to fit your unique vision and budget.
